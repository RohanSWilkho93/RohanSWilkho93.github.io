Flood Finder 360

FloodFinder360 is an innovative software designed for analyzing historical flash flood events in the U.S. It utilizes advanced NLP and Machine Learning algorithms to enhance information retrieval, achieving a 63% performance improvement over traditional search engines. This tool aids in understanding disaster patterns and developing proactive mitigation strategies. This tool is available here

Platform for Resilience Inference Measurement and Enhancement (PRIME)

PRIME is a cutting-edge platform on CyberGISx that employs machine learning for an objective, data-driven index to assess community resilience against disasters. It integrates socio-economic factors, offering accurate regional resilience analysis and highlighting crucial socio-economic influencers. This tool sets new benchmarks in disaster preparedness, aiding in effective response strategies and fostering community resilience.

Dynamic Flash Flood Susceptibility (DFFS)

DFFS is an advanced GIS-based software incorporating machine learning, designed to tackle the unique challenges of flash floods in the U.S. It analyzes and understands factors contributing to these region-specific disasters. Offering more than just data analysis, DFFS provides customized assessments of community susceptibility, delivering region-specific insights for flash flood prediction and preparedness. This tool is a significant technological breakthrough, enhancing community resilience and reducing risks associated with these unpredictable natural disasters.
