GIS Tool for Dynamic Assessment of Community Flash Flood Susceptibility

In the US, flash floods are notorious as the leading cause of fatalities from natural disasters, striking unexpectedly and with localized devastation. Our research addresses the unique challenge these floods present due to their rapid and regional-specific nature. We're spearheading the development of a GIS-based tool, integrating advanced machine learning, to dissect and understand the diverse factors that incite flash floods in various regions.

This innovative tool goes beyond mere data analysis; it offers a tailored assessment of community susceptibility to flash floods, considering the distinct characteristics of each region. By providing customized, region-specific insights, our approach revolutionizes flash flood prediction and community preparedness. It's not just a technological breakthrough but a crucial step towards empowering communities, enhancing their resilience, and significantly reducing the risks associated with these unpredictable natural disasters.
